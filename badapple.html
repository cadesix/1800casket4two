"bad apple"
by
MICHAEL CASPER
release date of july 14th, 2019

"when i wrote ‘bad apple’ a year ago i was at this place with my mental health where i was letting my mania take over everything in my life. almost every thought i had was a negative one and i was making my way into a world where the colors around me weren’t as vibrant anymore. i was anxious about being anxious which of course did nothing but make me more anxious. i would take my anti-psychotics on and off depending on if i was in the mood to feel suicidal or not. your perception starts to get all fucked up at a certain point. reality begins to blend with the stories you’ve created in your head. i just didn’t feel like i was enough, except when i was with u."
-michael casper
